# Radar Changelog

All notable releases. Detailed write-ups for each release live in [/build-log](build-log).

## V42 — Dual-Lane Funnel
May 3, 2026

Pivot to a dual-lane diagnostic system. The original archetype diagnostic lane (how you're being read) is joined by a scope diagnostic lane (how you're being valued). The full archetype diagnostic becomes free in exchange for account creation. Two new strategy session products introduced as the upsell tier above both lanes. See [v42-dual-lane-funnel.md](build-log/v42-dual-lane-funnel.md).

## V41 — Radar Decode
April 2026

Standalone $47 entry-tier product on a separate domain. Buyer sends a LinkedIn URL and a resume; receives a one-page diagnostic by hand within forty-eight hours. Bypasses the main platform's three earlier critiques (single-sentence overreach, no artifact parsing, no proof beat before the upsell) by being a parallel offer rather than a refactor. See [v41-radar-decode.md](build-log/v41-radar-decode.md).

## Archetype Intel Flywheel
May 3, 2026

Self-sharpening data layer. Every completed diagnostic feeds a population aggregate; every new diagnostic reads from that aggregate before generating output. Deterministic classification, scheduled aggregation, runtime injection. See [radar-archetype-intel-flywheel.md](build-log/radar-archetype-intel-flywheel.md).

## V40 — Funnel Friction Reduction
April 16, 2026

Friction reduction across the paid funnel. Name-only entry on the free diagnostic, results gated behind account creation, phone collection removed across paid flows. See [v40-funnel-friction.md](build-log/v40-funnel-friction.md).
