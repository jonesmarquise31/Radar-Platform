# Marquise Jones

**Date:** May 3, 2026

Founder of Workforce Radar.

Built Radar to diagnose how the modern hiring system reads or misreads skilled professionals. The platform began as a single archetype diagnostic, expanded into a two-axis diagnostic system across perception and valuation, and now operates a self-sharpening dataset that compounds across both axes. Every completed diagnostic improves the next one.

Three frameworks underpin the platform: the Career Chessboard (3C), the Abundance Mindset Reframe (AMR), and the diagnostic engine itself. Public versions of the first two are documented in this repository. The diagnostic engine — classification rules, scoring methodology, archetype patterns — stays private to full-engagement buyers and is not published.

Currently building Radar's distribution layer: recruiter-facing pool queries, coach-facing lead routing, and quarterly dataset reports drawn from the population. Each layer extends the dataset into a new audience without altering the diagnostic core that produces it.

Find Radar at [workforceradar.com](https://workforceradar.com). Long-form thinking on hiring systems and career intelligence on [LinkedIn](https://www.linkedin.com/in/jonesmarquise31).
