# Roadmap

**Date:** May 3, 2026

Forward-looking, intentionally directional. The platform's next development cycles sit in three buckets. Each bucket lists what is on the build list at the *what* level, not the *how* level. Quarter-specific commitments and revenue targets are deliberately excluded.

## Distribution Layer

Productize the dataset for parties beyond the diagnosed professional. The dataset has obvious value to audiences who currently have no structured access to it.

1. **Recruiter-facing pool queries.** Let recruiters query the classified pool by archetype cluster, scope verdict, target compensation, and target region. The pool surfaces professionals who match a recruiter's open role at a structural level rather than a keyword level.

2. **Coach-facing lead routing.** Connect career coaches to diagnosed professionals matching their specialty. Coaches subscribe to a slice of the pool; professionals see coaches positioned against their specific diagnostic result.

3. **Quarterly dataset reports.** Publish trend reports drawn from the population — what shifted in the dataset over the past quarter, where the misalignment is concentrating, which clusters are growing fastest. The reports become the platform's primary content distribution surface.

## Diagnostic Depth

Extend the diagnostic engine without increasing buyer friction. Each item below is additive — none changes the existing diagnostic experience for current buyers.

1. **Multi-segment aggregation.** Break the dataset into segments by industry, region, and experience tier. The aggregate becomes a matrix instead of a single row. Diagnoses can ground themselves in the segment the buyer sits inside, not just the global population.

2. **Longitudinal tracking.** Measure positioning shift over time for repeat buyers. The platform records each diagnostic against a buyer's history; the second diagnosis is read against the first, the third against the trajectory. Movement becomes a measurable output.

3. **Cross-axis correlation analysis.** Surface which archetype patterns correlate with which scope verdicts. The dataset already contains both axes per buyer; the correlation read becomes a diagnostic input rather than a separate analysis.

## Platform Operations

Tighten the platform's operating posture. Each item below is internal — the buyer does not see them directly, but the operator's ability to run the platform improves.

1. **Automated buyer comms layer.** Replace ad-hoc outreach with structured communication tied to diagnostic events. Welcome messages, follow-ups, and engagement prompts run on the platform's own timing rather than the operator's available bandwidth.

2. **Public stat block refresh on every aggregate update.** The platform's public homepage references the dataset's current state. Stat blocks refresh automatically as the aggregate refreshes, without operator intervention.

3. **Investor-facing dashboard view.** A read-only surface that exposes the operating numbers at the level an investor needs to track them. The same dataset that grounds diagnoses also grounds external accountability.
