# 002 — Account Creation as Currency

**Date:** May 3, 2026
**Status:** Accepted

## Context

The full archetype diagnostic was originally a paid product. Conversion against the free entry quiz was low. The diagnostic's primary value, examined honestly, sits more with the platform (dataset feeder, relationship anchor) than with the buyer (single-use written output). Pricing the diagnostic gated the asset that matters most to the platform behind a transaction the buyer was not consistently willing to make.

## Decision

Make the full archetype diagnostic free in exchange for account creation. The trade is deliberate: account creation captures email, creates a relationship that compounds over time, and lets the buyer return to their results. The diagnostic itself becomes a dataset feeder rather than a revenue tier.

Revenue moves up the ladder to the strategy session tier, which is structurally harder to commoditize.

## Consequences

- **Volume increases substantially.** Removing the price filter removes the primary friction at the conversion step. Submissions per week grow with no incremental marketing spend.
- **Email asset grows.** Every diagnostic completion produces an account, which produces an addressable contact for downstream offers and content distribution.
- **Revenue ladder shifts upward.** The platform's revenue layer is now the strategy session tier and the existing scope-side paid product. The diagnostic itself is a top-of-funnel asset, not a bottom-of-funnel one.
- **Trade-off acknowledged.** The filter effect of payment is lost. Account creation is a weaker filter than a $97 charge. Buyers who would have self-selected via the paid version now appear in the dataset alongside lower-intent buyers. The platform absorbs this by treating account creation as the qualifier, not payment.
